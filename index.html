Can I freeze soup? How long does it last?

Yes, soups freeze for up to 3 months in airtight containers.

What are some healthy chicken or beef soup recipes?

Chicken vegetable, beef and barley, and chicken tortilla soup with lean meats and low-sodium broth.

How can I cook potatoes healthily?

Bake, boil, or roast them. Avoid frying to reduce fat.

How do I keep chicken moist while cooking?

Marinate and cook at a low temperature. Ensure the internal temp reaches 165°F (75°C).

Which beef cuts are best for slow cooking?

Chuck roast, brisket, and short ribs work best for slow cooking.
